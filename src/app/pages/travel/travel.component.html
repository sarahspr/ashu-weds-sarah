Travel to India

It is highly recommended to arrive in India at least 4 - 5 days before the weekend of the wedding. This will allow you time to recover from jet lag and to shop around for wedding outfits if you would like to wear Indian style clothing to the wedding.

Passport

US Passport Website

Traveling to India requires both a passport and tourist visa. If you do not already have a passport or need to renew it, it is recommended to do so as early as possible. The current routine processing time is 10 - 13 weeks. The current processing time for an expedited application is 7 - 9 weeks. You must have a passport in order to both purchase airfare and apply for your tourist visa (see below).

Visa

Indian Tourist Visa Website

Traveling to India requires a tourist visa. You can book your airfare before applying for your visa. The minimum time to apply for the visa before travel is 3 business days but it is recommended to apply sooner in case of any delays. The entire visa process is online. You can apply for a 6 month tourist at the link below. It will take a couple of days to receive approval by email. You will need to print out the approval email and carry it with you along with your passport.

Please note: You will need a digital passport-sized photo (2x2). It should be in JPEG format with a minimum size 10 KB and a maximum size 1 MB. You will also need a Scanned page of your passport details (the page where your photo and information is listed). This should be a PDF with a minimum size of 10 KB and a maximum size of 300 KB.

Please don't hestitate to reach out if you have any questions about this process.

Airport Options

The closest airport to the venue is in a city called Chandigarh however this is a smaller domestic airport so you wil not be able to get a direct flight into Chandigarh.

The closest interational airport to the venue is Indira Gandhi International Airport in New Delhi. It is recommended to fly into New Delhi and then either book an Uber or take a domestic flight to Chandigarh.

You can book a same day intercity Uber as long as you pay with cash which including tolls will cost you around 3,500 Indian Rupees one way ($43) and will take around 4.5 hours. A roundtrip domestic flight between New Delhi (DEL) and Chandigarh (IXC) costs around $86 roundtrip and takes about 1 hour.
